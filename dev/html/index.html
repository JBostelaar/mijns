Party Inside a Glacier at Iceland’s Secret Solstice Festival

  • access_time 05 december 2015
  • chat_bubble_outline 0 comments

Ever attend a massive warehouse rave and think how anyone could possibly top it? Well this year’s Secret Solstice Festival certainly will be, as they plan to host the world’s very first party within a glacier as part of the festival’s extended off-site bespoke celebrations. Limited to only 70 guests, the once in a lifetime experience will take place inside Langjökull – Europe’s second-largest glacier – located in the wilder outskirts of Reykjavík, Iceland. The lucky guests will be some of the first people on Earth to descend into a man-made cave system beneath ice up to 580m thick.
